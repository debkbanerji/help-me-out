About Service Ninja

Service Ninja is an application that connects people with the services that they need, by sorting them based on priority. The priority is calculated by taking into account both the time of the request and the urgency of the request using a geometric progression.


Each level of urgency adds to the time of the request a different offset before sorting

Critical: 30 minutes

Very Urgent: 1 hour

Urgent: 2 hours

Important: 4 hours

Take Your Time: 8 hours


This app was made for 'Midnight Madness' at HackGSU 2017

It was made within a time limit of 4 hours
